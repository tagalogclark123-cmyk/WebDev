About Harry Potter

The Harry Potter series is a spellbinding collection of fantasy novels by J.K. Rowling. It follows the journey of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his magical years at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Across 7 books and 8 films, fans have discovered the enchanting world of magic, courage, friendship, and the battle against the Dark Lord.
